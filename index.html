Meet Betsy Mendel!

Betsy is a member of both the Author's Guild and the Writer's Gulld of America, and her work has been featured in prestigious publications such as the LA Times, The Santa Monica Mirror, The Jewish Journal, The Argonaut, and the Beverly Hills Courier. Originally from Atlanta, GA, she now calls sunny Santa Monica, CA home, where she lives with her husband Rick and their feline overlord, Patches. Betsy's passion lies in empowering ochers to break free from mediocrity and embrace an amazingly awesome life! Betsy can be reached on IG @Betsymendel.
